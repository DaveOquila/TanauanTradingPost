ABOUT

Get to know Tanauan Trading Post.

A Trading Post is a station or store of a trader or trading company established in a thinly settled region where local products (as furs) are exchanged for manufactured goods.

Examples of trading posts of goods include farmer's markets, flea markets, antique stores, consignment shops, online marketplaces such as Amazon and eBay, and specialized trading platforms such as Alibaba for wholesale goods.
